# Ecommerce Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Ecommerce Sales Dashboard** is a data analytics project developed using **Power BI** to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product categories, and profit trends in an e-commerce business.

This interactive dashboard provides insights into **total sales amount, product quantities sold, profit generated, payment methods used by customers, and sales performance across states and categories**.

The project demonstrates how **business intelligence and data visualization tools can help businesses track performance and make data-driven decisions**.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze **overall sales performance of the e-commerce business**
* Identify **top performing states based on sales amount**
* Study **product category sales distribution**
* Analyze **monthly profit trends**
* Identify **top customers based on purchase amount**
* Understand **customer payment preferences**
* Analyze **profit distribution across product sub-categories**

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### 1️⃣ Sales Amount by State

The bar chart visualizes the **total sales amount generated by different states**.

States included:

* Maharashtra
* Madhya Pradesh
* Uttar Pradesh
* Delhi
* Rajasthan

**Key Insight**

* Maharashtra generates the **highest sales amount**, indicating strong customer demand in this region.

---

### 2️⃣ Quantity by Product Category

The donut chart shows the **distribution of product quantities sold across categories**.

Categories include:

* Clothing (63%)
* Electronics (21%)
* Furniture (16%)

**Key Insight**

* Clothing dominates sales quantity, indicating **high demand for fashion products**.

---

### 3️⃣ Monthly Profit Analysis

The bar chart visualizes **profit generated across different months**.

**Key Insight**

* Profit fluctuates throughout the year, with some months showing **higher sales activity and profit generation**.

---

### 4️⃣ Sales by Customer

The chart highlights **top customers based on total purchase amount**.

Examples include:

* Harivansh
* Shiva
* Madhav
* Vrinda
* Lalita
* Priyanka

**Key Insight**

* A small group of customers contributes significantly to total sales.

---

### 5️⃣ Quantity by Payment Mode

The donut chart analyzes **customer payment preferences**.

Payment modes include:

* Cash on Delivery (COD) – 44%
* UPI – 20%
* Debit Card – 14%
* Credit Card – 12%
* EMI – 11%

**Key Insight**

* **Cash on Delivery remains the most preferred payment method**, followed by digital payments.

---

### 6️⃣ Profit by Sub-Category

The bar chart highlights **profit generated by different product sub-categories**.

Sub-categories include:

* Printers
* Bookcases
* Phones
* Saree
* Trousers

**Key Insight**

* Some product sub-categories generate significantly **higher profits compared to others**.
