# AI Job Market Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **AI Job Market Analysis Dashboard** is a data analytics project developed using **Power BI** to analyze global salary trends, AI adoption levels, company size distribution, and job growth projections in the Artificial Intelligence industry.

This interactive dashboard helps understand how **AI adoption, company size, and geographic locations influence salary distribution in the AI job market**.

The project demonstrates how **data visualization and business intelligence tools** can transform complex datasets into clear and actionable insights.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze **AI salary distribution across different global locations**
* Study how **company size impacts salary distribution**
* Understand the **relationship between AI adoption levels and salary trends**
* Identify **overall salary distribution in the AI industry**
* Visualize **job growth projections in the AI job market**
* Build an **interactive dashboard using Power BI**

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### 1️⃣ Salary Distribution by Location

The bar chart visualizes the **sum of salaries across major global cities**.

#### Locations Included

* San Francisco
* Singapore
* Sydney
* Tokyo
* New York
* Dubai
* Berlin
* Paris
* London
* Toronto

**Key Insight**

* Global technology hubs like **San Francisco, Singapore, and New York** show higher salary distributions due to strong demand for AI professionals.

---

### 2️⃣ Salary Distribution by Company Size

The donut chart shows how salaries are distributed across companies of different sizes.

* **Small Companies** – 15.8M (35%)
* **Medium Companies** – 14.8M (32%)
* **Large Companies** – 15.0M (33%)

**Key Insight**

* Salary distribution across **small, medium, and large companies is relatively balanced**, showing AI demand across all types of organizations.

---

### 3️⃣ Salary Distribution by AI Adoption Level

This chart highlights salary distribution based on **AI adoption levels in organizations**.

#### AI Adoption Categories

* High AI Adoption
* Medium AI Adoption
* Low AI Adoption

**Key Insight**

* Companies with **higher AI adoption contribute significantly to the overall salary pool**.

---

### 4️⃣ AI Job Growth Projection

The waterfall chart illustrates the **future projection of AI job market growth**.

#### Growth Phases

* Decline Phase
* Growth Phase
* Stable Phase
* Total Projection

**Key Insight**

* Despite short-term fluctuations, the **AI job market shows strong long-term growth potential**.
