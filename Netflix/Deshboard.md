# Netflix Movies & TV Shows Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Netflix Movies & TV Shows Analysis Dashboard** is a data analytics project developed using **Power BI** to explore and analyze the Netflix content library.

This interactive dashboard provides insights into **total titles, genres, ratings, countries producing Netflix content, and trends in movie and TV show releases over time**.

The project demonstrates how **data visualization techniques can transform entertainment industry data into meaningful insights**.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze the **total number of movies and TV shows available on Netflix**
* Identify the **most popular genres on the platform**
* Study the **distribution of movies vs TV shows**
* Analyze **content release trends over the years**
* Identify **top countries producing Netflix content**
* Examine **content ratings distribution**

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### 1️⃣ Movies vs TV Shows Distribution

The donut chart shows the **distribution of content types on Netflix**.

* **Movies:** 6.13K (69.62%)
* **TV Shows:** 2.68K (30.38%)

**Key Insight**

* Movies dominate the Netflix platform compared to TV shows.

---

### 2️⃣ Content Release Trend by Year

The line chart visualizes the **number of movies and TV shows released each year**.

**Key Insight**

* The number of Netflix releases increased significantly after **2015**, reflecting the rapid expansion of the platform.

---

### 3️⃣ Genre Distribution

The genre chart highlights the **most popular content genres** on Netflix.

Popular genres include:

* Dramas
* International Movies
* Documentaries
* Stand-Up Comedy
* Comedies
* Kids TV
* Children & Family Movies

**Key Insight**

* Drama and international content dominate Netflix's catalog.

---

### 4️⃣ Top Countries Producing Netflix Content

The bar chart shows the **top countries contributing the most Netflix titles**.

Some major countries include:

* United States
* India
* United Kingdom
* Canada
* Japan
* Spain
* Mexico

**Key Insight**

* The United States contributes the largest share of Netflix content.

---

### 5️⃣ Content Rating Distribution

The rating chart shows how Netflix content is categorized based on **viewer rating categories**.

Examples of ratings include:

* TV-MA
* TV-14
* TV-PG
* R
* PG-13
* TV-Y7
* PG
* TV-G

**Key Insight**

* Most Netflix content falls under **TV-MA and TV-14**, indicating a large amount of mature and teen audience content.
